# Design System Guide (DESIGN.md)

This document outlines the design principles, color palettes, and typography for our new projects.

---

## 🏋️ PowerTrack (Workout App)
*Premium, high-energy, and data-driven.*

### Design Tokens
- **Primary Color**: `#137FEC` (Electric Blue)
- **Secondary Color**: `#0A0A0A` (Deep Space Black)
- **Theme**: Dark Mode
- **Typography**: Inter / Space Grotesk (Modern Sans)
- **Roundness**: 12px (Smooth edges for premium feel)

### UI Components
- **Glassmorphism Cards**: Used for stats and lists to create depth.
- **Gradient Charts**: Vibrant blue-to-purple gradients for progress visualization.
- **Neon Accents**: High-contrast icons and buttons for clear CTAs.

---

## 🏯 K-Journey (Korea Intro & Travel)
*Elegant, editorial, and traditional-modern fusion.*

### Design Tokens
- **Primary Color**: `#D4AF37` (Pale Gold)
- **Secondary Color**: `#FDFCF8` (Soft Cream Beige)
- **Theme**: Light Mode
- **Typography**: Noto Serif (Traditional) / Plus Jakarta Sans (Modern)
- **Roundness**: 8px (Balanced and organic)

### UI Components
- **Hanok-inspired Motifs**: Subtle traditional borders and textures on card corners.
- **Full-Bleed Imagery**: Large, high-quality landscape photos for immersive experience.
- **Editorial Layouts**: Generous whitespace and sophisticated typography for a luxury feel.
